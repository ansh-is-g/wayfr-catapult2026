# Personas Page — 30s Demo Script

"Personas lets you tell wayfr who you are — so it can show you what matters to you.

I'll type 'I'm a firefighter doing a building survey.' Under the hood, two specialized AI subagents kick off: the first one understands your role and streams back a full persona profile in real time — needs, annotation focus, custom color scheme. The second agent takes that profile, runs it against every object in the 3D scene, and generates a completely personalized annotation plan.

You can edit anything before confirming — then the scene re-renders through your lens. Same space, totally different information layer.

That's Personas."
